Kasempa Youth Development Organization

WHO WE ARE AND WHAT DO WE DO?

KDYD was established in 2010 but got formal registration as an autonomous non-governmental organisation (NGO) in 2015 and holds a registration certificate under the registrar of societies. It was therefore founded with the vision to bringing sustainable livelihood to the youth and vulnerable through life changing skills, good health and other community challenges faced by orphans, women and the different abbled community members

MAIN OBJECTIVE OF THE ORGANIZATION

The main objective of the organisation is to fight against HIV/AIDs, TB and Mental Health and provide support to the disabled, orphans, women, and the vulnerable and empower youths with survival skills for self sustainability.
